About



Learning to code is uniquely like gaining a superpower: it converts you from a consumer to a creator. Suddenly, computers become a tool for creating and youkiip as a software company is building a conglomerate of young developers who will master this art of using computers to develop real life products.

Make and Earn, from anywhere.
There’s never been a better time for making: anywhere in the world, anyone with a laptop and an internet connection can learn to make an app. Building things has never been so globally democratized. And that's why Youkiip has placed its axis towards Teens which are the future to enhance their super abilities in coding whilst providing them with Gigs which would aid them Earn while they study..

Our Target Audience
The goal of Youkiip is to make over 10 million teens professional developers which would be gainfully employed or even running their own startups, we are not just centered towards teaching and Mentoring, we are also concerned about the financial state of these young agile developers which would aid them in their educational pursuit and personal life. we target indivduals from ages 10-22 years and place more emphasis on low-income communities and educational deficient communities in Africa as a whole.
